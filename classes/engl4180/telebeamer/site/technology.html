TeleBeamer™ Model A Technology

The TeleBeamer™ Model A can transport any non-living matter anywhere on the planet, instantly. It uses a new technology developed by Enterprise Technologies (ET). This technology is called Photon Projection.

Photon Projection

Photon Projection uses super-accelerated light particles (photons) to disassemble an object’s atoms. It then beams those atoms to another location and reassembles them in exactly the same condition as before the process.

The technology that makes this possible was developed by ET over a period of 15 years and is safe and reliable. The secret to successful photon projection is ET’s proprietary supercomputer technology, ETNIAC.

ETNIAC

The Enterprise Technical Network Information Assembly Calculator (ETNIAC), is the most powerful supercomputer in the world. It can make 14 billion calculations a second. It can sort the atoms of an object and send them to any target on the planet. It is so intelligent it can calculate all variables for any possibilities. This ensures that every TeleBeamer™ shipment arrives in perfect condition, every time.

The TeleBeamer™ locates the precise target of your choosing, using the Enterprise Technologies Positioning Satellite System (ETPSS).

ETPSS

The ETPSS is a series of 5,000 global positioning satellites. The large number of satellites is for redundancy. Meaning that if 4,999 satellites malfunctioned, your delivery would still get to its destination safely and instantly. If all the satellites malfunctioned the TeleBeamer™ Model A safely and automatically restores the delivery to the point of origin. There is no possibility of lost or damaged materials. That’s the ET Guarantee.
