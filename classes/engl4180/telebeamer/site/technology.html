TeleBeamer Model A Technology

The TeleBeamer Model A can transport any non-living matter anywhere on the planet, instantly. It uses a new technology developed by Enterprise Technologies (ET). This technology is called photon Projection.

Photon projection

Photon projection uses super-accelerated light particles (photons) to disassemble an object’s atoms. It then beams those atoms to another location and reassembles them in exactly the same condition as before the process.

The technology that makes this possible was developed by ET over a period of 15 years and is safe and reliable. The secret to successful photon projection is ET’s proprietary supercomputer technology, ETNIAC.

ETNIAC

ETNIAC, short for Enterprise Technical Network Information Assembly Calculator, is the most powerful supercomputer in the world. It can make 14 billion calculations a second. It can sort the atoms of an object and send them to any target on the planet. It is so intelligent it can calculate any and all variables for any possibilities. This makes every TeleBeamer shipment arrive in perfect condition, every time. It calculates all forms of atmospheric interference instantly; making every shipment perfect.

It locates any precise target of your choosing using, the Enterprise Technologies Positioning Satellite (ETPS).

ETPS

The ETPS is a series of 5,000 global positioning satellites. The large number of satellites is for redundancy. Meaning that if 4,999 satellites malfunctioned, your delivery would still get were it’s supposed to be safely and instantly. If all the satellites malfunctioned the TeleBeamer model A automatically restores the delivery to the point of origin safely. There is no possibility of lost or damaged materials. That’s the ET Guarantee.
