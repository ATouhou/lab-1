Reviews and More...

Band - Camera Obscura
Title - Biggest Bluest Hi-Fi
Label - Andmoresound
Year - 2001

Review - Not just another twee Belle and Sebastian sound alike. Sad bastard music is something we like and this band doesn't disappoint. Imagine Belle and Sebastian with a girl voice. Then start going to all the hard to find music placesand start asking around. I found my copy at Mod Lang in Berkeley. Did I mention Stuart Murdoch has a production credit on this album? Do NOT confuse with a crap American band by the same name.

Band - White Stripes
Title - White Blood Cells
Label - Sympathy for the Record Industry http://www.whitestripes.com
Year - 2001

Review - Unless you live under a rock you have heard about White Stripes. A band comprised of brother and sister Jack and Meg White. Guitar and Drums, respectively. Do not pass them up, they are so stripped down, so true to rock-n-roll. Defintely run don't walk.

************************
Artist - Ian Brown
Title - Music of the Spheres
Label - Polydor http://www.ianbrown-online.co.uk
Year - 2001

Review - Ian Brown (former Stone Roses frontman) releases another excellent solo album. He still knows how to write a good groove, so his style remains fundamentally the same. The music tends to be the most electronica yet.

Artist - Manu Chao
Title - Clandestino
Label - Ark21 http://www.ark21.com
Year - 1998

Review - People call it Latin pop, but is extremely infectious, and danceable. The lyrics are really good. Singing in Spanish, English and French. Most people like Bongo Bong, which is excellent, but I think Lagrimas De Oro is the best.
